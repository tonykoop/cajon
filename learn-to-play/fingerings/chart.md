# Cajon Strike-Zone Reference

```
╔══════════════════════════════════╗
║         TOP EDGE (slap zone)     ║
║  ┌──────────────────────────┐    ║
║  │  S  S  S  S  S  S  S  S │    ║
║  │  T  T  T  T  T  T  T  T │    ║
║  │                          │    ║
║  │       CENTER (bass)      │    ║
║  │           B              │    ║
║  │                          │    ║
║  │                          │    ║
║  └──────────────────────────┘    ║
║         BOTTOM EDGE              ║
╚══════════════════════════════════╝

  B = Palm center — full resonant bass tone
  S = Fingers strike top edge — bright crack
  T = Fingertips top edge — muted click
  H = Heel rests on tapa while fingers lift (dampening)
```

## Hand position
- Sit on the cajon with the playing face between your knees.
- Lean forward slightly so both hands fall naturally on the upper half.
- Bass strokes: right or left hand, palm flat, relax and rebound.
- Slap strokes: slightly cupped fingers, snap at the wrist, pull back fast.
