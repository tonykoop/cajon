# Shuffle Groove ★★☆

Swing eighths: the "+" beat falls closer to the next downbeat (triplet feel).
Play each "+" as the third triplet note of its beat.

```
Beat (swing):  1  (+) 2  (+) 3  (+) 4  (+)
               B   T  -   -  S   T  -   T
```

Heavier version:
```
Beat (swing):  1  (+) 2  (+) 3  (+) 4  (+)
               B   H  T   -  S   H  T   -
```

**Form:** 16-bar loop. Tempo: 80 BPM (swing feel).

**Practice tip:** Listen to a basic blues shuffle to internalize the triplet feel before playing.
