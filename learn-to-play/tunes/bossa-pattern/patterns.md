# Bossa Nova Pattern ★★★

The Brazilian bossa nova cajon groove uses all four stroke types.
Right hand plays the clave; left hand plays bass and heel fills.

```
Clave (R):  - S - - S - S - | - - S - S - - -
Bass  (L):  B - - B - - - - | B - - - B - B -
Heel  (L):  - - H - - H - H | - H - H - - - H
```

Combined (notate one bar at a time at 16th-note resolution):
```
Beat:  1  e  +  a  2  e  +  a  3  e  +  a  4  e  +  a
R:     -  S  -  -  S  -  S  -  -  -  S  -  S  -  -  -
L:     B  -  H  B  -  H  -  H  B  H  -  H  B  -  B  H
```

**Form:** 16-bar loop. Tempo: 120 BPM.

**Source:** Traditional bossa nova clave pattern, public domain.
