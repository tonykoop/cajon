# Flamenco Rumba Pattern ★★☆

Rumba clave over 4/4. The clave pattern is the rhythmic backbone.
Play it one hand while the other marks steady quarter notes.

```
Clave (right hand):
Beat:  1  e  +  a  2  e  +  a  3  e  +  a  4  e  +  a
Clave: S  -  -  S  -  -  S  -  -  -  S  -  S  -  -  -

Body (left hand, steady):
       B  -  -  -  B  -  -  -  B  -  -  -  B  -  -  -
```

Combined:
```
Beat:  1  e  +  a  2  e  +  a  3  e  +  a  4  e  +  a
R:     S  -  -  S  -  -  S  -  -  -  S  -  S  -  -  -
L:     B  -  -  -  B  -  -  -  B  -  -  -  B  -  -  -
```

**Form:** 8-bar loop repeated. Tempo: 100–130 BPM.

**Note:** In flamenco tradition, the palm of the right hand brushes the tapa edge on slap strokes.
