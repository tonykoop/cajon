# Cajon Warm-Up: Single-Stroke Builder

Practice each row at 60 BPM (one symbol = one quarter note beat) before increasing tempo.

```
Single bass:
| B - - - | B - - - | B - - - | B - - - |

Single slap:
| - - S - | - - S - | - - S - | - - S - |

Bass + slap:
| B - S - | B - S - | B - S - | B - S - |

All strokes:
| B T S T | B T S T | B T S T | B T S T |
```

**Tip:** Plant your strumming hand lightly on the tapa to feel the vibration.
Aim for a clean separation between the deep bass thud and the crisp slap crack.
