# Cajon Measurement Data

This folder is the landing zone for measured CJ-S prototype data.

Do not promote the packet beyond pre-build V5 candidate status until measured
rows exist in `../validation.csv` and source files are added here for the
critical gates:

- panel thickness after milling and after inlay routing;
- finger-joint scrap-fit and glue-line checks;
- shell diagonal/squareness and seat-load results;
- centre-strike and corner-strike audio captures or spectrum summaries;
- snare-onset notes for option B guitar-string snare;
- finish and tapa screw-torque observations.

Suggested file names:

- `cj-s-panel-thickness.csv`
- `cj-s-shell-squareness.csv`
- `cj-s-strike-spectrum.csv`
- `cj-s-snare-onset.md`
