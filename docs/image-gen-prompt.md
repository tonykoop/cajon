# Cajon Image-Gen Prompts

Concept visualization only; not fabrication authority. Do not infer panel
thickness, screw positions, snare geometry, CNC toolpaths, or tuning from these
prompts.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic 3/4 product photograph of a finished Standard cajon prototype from a parametric three-member family: a black walnut box drum with precise finger-joint corners, a thin Baltic-birch tapa front plate, a circular rear sound hole hinted by angle, and subtle maple plus yellowheart CNC inlay panels on the sides and back. The instrument should look like a refined furniture-grade percussion object, with rubber feet, clean tapa screws around the perimeter, and a warm satin finish. Clean studio background, soft directional lighting, realistic seated scale from a cushion or hands nearby, 50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic scene of the cajon being played in a small studio or woodshop performance corner: a seated player strikes the center of the tapa for bass while the upper corners suggest flamenco snare slap, with the walnut body and side inlay visible. Include a tuner, guitar-string snare adjustment tool, and wood offcuts in the background. Natural light, believable seated posture, no stage smoke, intimate acoustic storytelling.

## Detail / Macro Prompt

Photorealistic macro close-up of the cajon's front upper corner: thin Baltic-birch tapa with evenly spaced screws, crisp finger-joint walnut corner, a glimpse of contrasting maple/yellowheart inlay on the side panel, and the subtle shadow where the tapa can vibrate. Show satin finish, real wood pores, screw heads, and clean joinery. High-detail macro, shallow depth of field.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, logos, watermarks, fake brand badges, impossible box joints, extra limbs, distorted hands, visible dimension callouts, or random snare wires outside the box.
